Border-image

border-image: url(border.png) 30 round;

Border-image-slice

border-image: url(border.png) 30 round;

Border-image-slice %

border-image: url(border.png) 30% round;

Border-image-repeat

border-image: url(border.png) 30 stretch;
  • stretch - The image is stretched to fill the area
  • repeat - The image is tiled (repeatd) to fill the area
  • round - The image is tiled (repeated) to fill the area. If it does not fill the area with a whole number of tiles, the image is rescaled so it fits
  • space - The image is tiled (repeated) to fill the area. If it does not fill the area with a whole number of tiles, the extra space is distributed around the tiles
  • initial
  • inherit

Border-image-outset

Offset the border outside, can use px (length), number (ex 1 mean offset 1 boder)

Border-image-width

The width of image, can use px (length), number (ex 2 mean double initial width), % (ex 10% 20% mean 10% of horizontal area, 20% of vertial area)
